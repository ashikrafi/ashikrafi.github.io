Md Ashikur Rahman is a Lead AI Engineer at The KOW Company, where he leads 20+ engineers building production vision-language and LLM systems. He is an ICDAR 2026 accepted author on grounding vision-language models for diagram understanding.

Bio: He works across AI research, software engineering, and product development, taking ideas from problem formulation to publication and deployment. His research is accompanied by open-source code, datasets, and models on GitHub and Hugging Face. Before specializing in AI, he spent five years as a software engineer building large-scale production systems, which helps him bridge research and engineering in practice.

Research Interest: His work spans AI interpretability, multimodal learning, computer vision, and large language models, with a focus on vision-language model reliability, visual grounding, prompt safety, and 3D reconstruction. He is particularly interested in building efficient, reliable AI systems that move from research prototypes to production platforms serving global e-commerce and enterprise clients.
